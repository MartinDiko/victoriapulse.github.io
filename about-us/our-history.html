Leading in Pulse Trading &
Sustainable Agriculture

Victoria Pulse Trading Corporation, established in 2002, has become a cornerstone in the global pulse trade, leveraging Canada's rich agricultural heritage. Our commitment to sustainability, quality, and strong relationships with Saskatchewan's pulse growers allows us to supply premium pulses to the world. Our journey from a family-owned operation to an international trading powerhouse mirrors our dedication to excellence and integrity in every transaction.
